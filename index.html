ABOUT ME

When I was a kid, I would marvel at the ability to communicate and share information over the internet. I was interested in early online messaging platforms like AIM and MSN Messenger, as well as looking up my favorite bands' websites.

Fast forward, 20 years later, I was now in a band that needed a website. With the 2020 global pandemic, I found I had more time on my hands. I decided to put that time to good use and built purebrown.net with no previous experience.

I just finished the 'Intro to Web Development' course at the AIM Code School with a certificate of completion. I enjoy working with computers, playing music and challenging myself.
